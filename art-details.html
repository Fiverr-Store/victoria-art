Art By Victoria-n

Victorine Ngangu’s artworks are “The Gifts of Life” as she calls them. Everything she creates with her hands always turns magical including cooking. Her first artworks were done several years ago and are online for sale now. Her recent paintings, which are not online or for sale yet, are even more impressive than her previous artworks. They will be sold at the Gallery. The artworks sold online now are to help the street homeless children of Africa. The pieces are in excellent condition. All her printmaking and painting arts are original.
